"Our Specials"

Our paneer tikka is a classic Indian appetizer that's perfect for vegetarians and meat lovers alike. Made with soft and creamy paneer cheese, marinated in a blend of aromatic spices and yogurt, then grilled to perfection in a traditional tandoor oven. Served with a side of mint chutney and a wedge of lemon, this dish is a perfect combination of spicy and tangy flavors. Our paneer tikka is not only delicious but also nutritious, as paneer is a great source of protein and calcium. Experience the melt-in-your-mouth texture of this flavorful dish, and let the spices tantalize your taste buds.
