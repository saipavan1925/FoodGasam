Dosa

A crispy South Indian crepe-like pancake made from a fermented batter of rice and lentils, with a tangy flavor and light, airy texture. Served with your choice of accompaniment, including sambar, coconut chutney, spicy tomato chutney, or onion chutney. This gluten-free and low-fat dish is not only delicious but also nutritious, providing a good source of carbohydrates, proteins, and essential vitamins and minerals. Try one of our variations, including Masala Dosa, Rava Dosa, or Cheese Dosa, for a unique and flavorful twist on this classic dish.
