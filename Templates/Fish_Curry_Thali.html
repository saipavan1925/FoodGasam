"Our Specials"

Indulge in a seafood lover's delight with our fish curry thali. This authentic South Indian dish features tender fish pieces simmered in a spicy and tangy tomato-based gravy, infused with a blend of aromatic spices. Our thali comes with a serving of steaming hot basmati rice, crispy papadum, a tangy lime pickle, and a side of creamy raita. Finish off your meal with a sweet and decadent serving of traditional South Indian dessert, such as payasam or gulab jamun. This dish is not only delicious but also nutritious, as fish is a good source of lean protein and omega-3 fatty acids.
